About Me

14 year old 8th grader at Rise Academy

My name is Wafi Syed, a middle schooler with a passion for computer science, and sports. The first traces of me coding date back to around 2017-2018 on Scratch, where I watched my Aunt, Najiah Sabzwari, who was in highschool at the time, make a game where a witch would fly around collecting ghosts. When she asked me to try it, I was happy to, and I was amazed at how the game was made. From that point on I was eager to learn how to create my own games and websites. And after 6 years, here I am. Making the world a better place, line by line.
